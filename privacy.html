Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2025

Sima Swipe Calculator is a lightweight calculator app designed for quick access through a floating overlay. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to make it clear that:

  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
  • All calculations and app usage happen entirely on your device.
  • The only permission required is “Appear on top”, which is strictly used to display the calculator overlay above other apps.

Because no data is collected, there is nothing to delete or manage — your activity stays private on your own device.
